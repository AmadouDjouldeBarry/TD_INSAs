Tourist Sites in Zanzibar

Stone Town

Stone Town is the old part of Zanzibar City, the main city of Zanzibar. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its unique architecture, which reflects the diverse influences underlying the Swahili culture.

Prison Island

Prison Island, also known as Changuu Island, is a small island off the coast of Zanzibar. It is known for its giant Aldabra tortoises and its historic prison ruins.

Jozani Forest

Jozani Forest is the only national park in Zanzibar. It is home to the endemic Zanzibar red colobus monkey and a variety of other wildlife.

Nungwi Beach

Nungwi Beach is located at the northern tip of Zanzibar. It is known for its beautiful white sand beaches, clear blue waters, and vibrant marine life.

Kizimkazi

Kizimkazi is a small fishing village on the southern coast of Zanzibar. It is famous for its dolphin tours and historic mosque.
